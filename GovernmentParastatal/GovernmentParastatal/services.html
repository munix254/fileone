MINISTRY OF TOURISM

Services

  1. The following are functions of the Ministry and Services offered by the Ministry:-
  2. Formulation of national tourism policies, plans, strategies, guidelines and goals
  3. Development and Promotion of Tourism
  4. Wildlife Conservation, Research and protection Education and Awareness
  5. Training on Tourism Services
  6. Tourism Financing
  7. Protection of Wildlife Heritage
  8. Wildlife Biodiversity Management and Protection
  9. Management of National Parks and Game reserves and Marine Parks
  10. Collaboration with Wildlife clubs of Kenya
  11. . Management of Wildlife Dispersal areas in collaboration with partners
  12. . Development of Legislation and enforcement of Standards and Quality
  13. Provision of Incentives to boost businesses in the tourism sector
  14. Job creation
  15. Provide technical assistance and advisory services to entities engaged in tourism product development including County Governments, investors, community tourism enterprises and M/SMEs in tourism
  16. Adoption of innovation
  17. Communication and Information on tourism
  18. Open new areas for tourism development
  19. Establish and facilitate an enabling environment for promotion of local and foreign investment in tourism
  20. Sustainable management of tourism for posterity
